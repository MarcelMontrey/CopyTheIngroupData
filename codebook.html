Codebook for Copy-the-Ingroup

Authors

Marcel Montrey & Thomas R. Shultz

Description

This codebook describes the format and interpretation of the variables in the attached data.

Files

exp1-raw-full.tsv

Contains the raw data from experiment 1. Analysis was not performed on these files directly.
  • subject: Participant ID (indexed from 0). No IDs are assigned to those excluded from the analysis (the first generation).
  • chain: Transmission chain ID (indexed from 0).
  • generation: Experimental generation (indexed from 0).
  • index: Participant's index within their chain/generation (indexed from 0).
  • age: Age (in years).
  • sex: Sex (Male or Female).
  • group: Assigned group (0 or 1).
  • rabbit: Nest the rabbit inhabits (0 or 1), across 20 rounds.
  • machine: Nest the rabbit-finding machine would indicate as the correct one (0 or 1), if the machine was used.
  • scores: Previous generation's scores after each block of five rounds. Individuals' scores are delimited by commas and blocks are delimited by semi-colons. Values of -1 indicate that no score was observed.
  • pre: Pre-game rating of the ingroup's relative reliability.
  • post: Post-game rating of the ingroup's relative reliability.
  • chosen: Each observed individual's index within their chain/generation (0 to 5). Indices are delimited by commas and rounds are delimited by semi-colons. Values of -1 indicate that no one was observed.
  • chosenInput: Nest selected by each observed individual (0 or 1). Guesses are delimited by commas and rounds are delimited by semi-colons. Values of -1 indicate that no one was observed.
  • useMachine: Whether the participant chose to use the rabbit-finding machine (0 or 1), across 20 rounds.
  • output: Participant's guesses about which nest the rabbit inhabits (0 or 1), across 20 rounds.
  • score: Participant's score, across 20 rounds.
  • gameTime: Time spent completing each round (in seconds).
  • IP_Hub_Country_Code: IP address' country of origin, according to IP Hub.
  • IP_Hub_ISP: IP address' ISP, according to IP Hub.
  • IP_Hub_nonUSIP: Whether this IP address originates from outside the U.S., according to IP Hub.
  • IP_Hub_VPS: Whether this IP address originates from a VPS, according to IP Hub.
  • IP_Hub_recommend_block: Whether this IP address originates from outside the U.S. or from a VPS, according to IP Hub.

exp2-raw-full.tsv

Contains the raw data from experiment 2. Analysis was not performed on these files directly.
  • subject, generation, index, age, sex, group, rabbit, machine, scores, chosen, chosenInput, useMachine, output, score, and gameTime are the same as in exp1-raw-data.tsv.
  • condition: Condition ID (indexed from 0).
  • pre: Pre-game ratings of the ingroup's competence, capability, warmth, and friendliness, followed by the outgroup's competence, capability, warmth, and friendliness.
  • post: Post-game ratings, in the same order.
  • surveyTime: Time spent completing the pre-game and post-game group stereotype questionnaires (in seconds).

exp1-wide.tsv

Contains the processed data from experiment 1 in wide format.
  • subject, chain, generation, age, sex, group, pre, post, IP_Hub_Country_Code, IP_Hub_ISP, IP_Hub_nonUSIP, IP_Hub_VPS, and IP_Hub_recommend_block are the same as in exp1-raw-data.tsv.
  • totalGameTime: Total time spent playing the game (in seconds).
  • finalScore: Final score (in points).
  • il: Number of times the participant used the rabbit-finding machine (individual learning).
  • agreeIG: Number of agreements with ingroup members.
  • agreeOG: Number of agreements with outgroup members.
  • agreeTotal: Total number of agreements (ingroup plus outgroup)
  • agreeBias: Which group was copied more often (ingroup bias, outgroup bias, or unbiased).
  • agreeMaxIG: Number of opportunities the participant had to agree with the ingroup, given whom they observed.
  • agreeMaxOG: Number of opportunities the participant had to agree with the outgroup, given whom they observed.
  • obsIG: Number of observations of ingroup members.
  • obsOG: Number of observations of outgroup members.
  • obsTotal: Total number of observations (ingroup plus outgroup).
  • obsBias: Which group was observed more often (ingroup bias, outgroup bias, or unbiased).
  • hammingIG: Total Hamming distance from ingroup members in the previous generation.
  • hammingOG: Total Hamming distance from outgroup members in the previous generation.
  • hammingDelta: Difference in Hamming distance from ingroup and outgroup members (ingroup minus outgroup).

exp1-wide-full.tsv

Identical to exp1-wide.tsv, except it also includes data from participants who were excluded from the analysis (the first generation).

exp2-wide.tsv

Contains the processed data from experiment 2 in wide format.
  • subject, generation, age, sex, group, totalGameTime, finalScore, il, agreeIG, agreeOG, agreeTotal, agreeBias, agreeMaxIG, agreeMaxOG, obsIG, obsOG, obsTotal, obsBias, hammingIG, hammingOG, and hammingDelta are the same as in exp1-wide.tsv.
  • condition is the same as in exp2-raw-full.tsv.
  • models: Number of individuals observed per round (behavioral models).
  • preCompIG: Pre-game competence rating of the ingroup (average of "competence" and "capability").
  • preCompOG: Pre-game competence rating of the outgroup.
  • preCompDelta: Difference between the groups' pre-game competence ratings (ingroup minus outgroup).
  • preCompBias: Which group the pre-game competence rating favors (ingroup bias, outgroup bias, or unbiased).
  • preWarmIG: Pre-game warmth rating of the ingroup (average of "warmth" and "friendliness").
  • preWarmOG: Pre-game warmth rating of the outgroup.
  • preWarmDelta: Difference between the groups' pre-game warmth ratings.
  • preWarmBias: Which group the pre-game warmth rating favors.
  • postCompIG: Post-game competence rating of the ingroup.
  • postCompOG: Post-game competence rating of the outgroup.
  • postCompDelta: Difference between the groups' post-game competence ratings.
  • postCompBias: Which group the post-game competence rating favors.
  • postWarmIG: Post-game warmth rating of the ingroup.
  • postWarmOG: Post-game warmth rating of the outgroup.
  • postWarmDelta: Difference between the groups' post-game warmth ratings.
  • postWarmBias: Which group the post-game warmth rating favors.
  • compDelta: Change in the ingroup competence bias (delta) over the course of the game (post-game bias minus pre-game bias).
  • warmDelta: Change in the ingroup warmth bias (delta) over the course of the game.

exp2-wide-full.tsv

Identical to exp2-wide.tsv, except it also includes data from participants who were excluded from the analysis (the first generation).

exp1-long.tsv

Contains the processed data from experiment 1 in long format (i.e., one entry for behavior toward the ingroup and one entry for behavior toward the outgroup).
  • subject, chain, generation, pre, post, agreeTotal, agreeBias, obsTotal, obsBias, and IP_Hub_recommend_block are the same as in exp1-wide.tsv.
  • group: Group in question (ingroup or outgroup).
  • agree: Number of agreements with this group's members.
  • agreeMax: Number of opportunities the participant had to agree with this group's members, given whom they observed.
  • obs: Number of observations of this group's members.
  • hamming: Total Hamming distance from this group's members in the previous generation.

exp2-long.tsv

Contains the processed data from experiment 2 in long format (i.e., one entry for behavior toward the ingroup and one entry for behavior toward the outgroup).
  • subject, generation, group, agree, agreeTotal, agreeBias, agreeMax, obs, obsTotal, obsBias, and hamming are the same as in exp1-long.tsv.
  • models, preCompDelta, preCompBias, preWarmDelta, preWarmBias, postCompDelta, postCompBias, postWarmDelta and postWarmBias are the same as in exp2-wide.tsv.
